Why This Exists

Every time you ask a friend to visit your city, they spend on travel, food, stays, and local services.
That money fuels the city's economy.

Backpack exists to reward that simple act.

If you bring someone to your city, you deserve something back.
And your friend deserves a more affordable trip.

About rewards

Rewards are paid manually after verification.
Amounts may vary and are limited during this early version of Backpack.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I verify my visit?

Upload a clear image or PDF of your flight or train ticket. Verification is done manually.

When do I receive the reward?

After manual verification, you'll receive a WhatsApp message confirming your reward.

What kind of reward is this?

Cash-based rewards sent directly after approval.

Is my data safe?

Yes. Your ticket is used only for verification and not stored after approval.

Can I upload a ticket booked earlier?

Yes, as long as the travel date is upcoming or recent and matches the visit.
