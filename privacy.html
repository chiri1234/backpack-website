Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

1. Keep it Simple & Honest

This is an early pilot program. We believe in transparency and minimizing the data we collect.

2. What Data We Collect

  • Contact Info: Name, Phone Number (for rewards), Email.
  • Verification Data: Uploaded Travel Tickets (Flight/Train/Bus).

3. Why We Collect It

We only use your data to:

  • Verify your visit to Bangalore.
  • Send you your cash reward manually via WhatsApp or UPI.
  • Prevent fraud and duplicate claims.

4. What We DO NOT Do

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties or advertisers. Your data stays with us.

5. How Data is Handled

During this early launch phase, data verification is handled manually by our trusted team to ensure accuracy. Once verified, we do not retain your ticket copies indefinitely.

6. Deletion & Contact

If you want us to delete your data at any time, simply email us at info@backpack.city and we will remove it immediately.
